# CareerCraft AI

**CareerCraft AI** is a specialized AI career advisor and resume coach for students in business, computer science, statistics, and data science. Built by Ibrahim Zeeshan on top of the [myAI3](https://github.com/dringel/myAI3) framework by Daniel Ringel.

## What It Does

CareerCraft AI helps students and recent graduates:

- **Resume writing & optimization** — tailored advice for business, CS, stats, and data science roles with ATS-beating strategies
- - **Cover letter coaching** — personalized, role-specific cover letters
  - - **LinkedIn profile optimization** — stand out to recruiters
    - - **Interview preparation** — behavioral, technical, and case interview coaching
      - - **Job & internship search strategy** — how to find, target, and apply effectively
        - - **Networking & cold outreach** — templates and strategies for reaching out to professionals
          - - **Salary negotiation** — how to evaluate offers and negotiate confidently
            - - **Career path guidance** — comparing roles (e.g., data analyst vs. data scientist vs. ML engineer), navigating dual-discipline backgrounds (CS + Business, Stats + Finance)
             
              - ## Target Audience
             
              - Undergraduate and graduate students in:
              - - Business (Finance, Marketing, Consulting, Operations, Analytics)
                - - Computer Science (SWE, Product, ML Engineering)
                  - - Statistics & Data Science
                   
                    - ## Tech Stack
                   
                    - - **Framework:** Next.js (deployed on Vercel)
                      - - **AI Model:** GPT-4.1 via OpenAI API
                        - - **Web Search:** Exa API
                          - - **Vector Database:** Pinecone (RAG)
                            - - **Content Moderation:** OpenAI Moderation API
                             
                              - ## Disclaimer
                             
                              - CareerCraft AI provides AI-generated career guidance for informational purposes only. Responses may be incomplete or imprecise. This tool does not constitute professional career counseling, legal employment advice, or guaranteed outcomes. Users should treat all suggestions as a starting point and consult their university career center or professional mentors for high-stakes decisions.
